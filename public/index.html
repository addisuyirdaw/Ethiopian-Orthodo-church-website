The Governance Model

Jurisdictional Hierarchy & Canonical Alignment

OrthodoxConnect encodes the full five-tier canonical authority structure of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church — from the Holy Synod Patriarchate to individual parishes — into a secure, auditable digital backbone.

The platform implements a multi-tenant jurisdictional model where each institution exists within a strict canonical scope. Every API call, every sacramental registration, and every financial transaction is governed by the ecclesiastical authority level of the authenticated user.

Sacramental records — baptisms, chrismations, marriages, and funerals — are cryptographically sealed with RSA-PSS-SHA256, linked to a dual Gregorian/Ethiopic calendar, and enforce canonical verification of clergy standing at the time of the ceremony.

The integrated FinTech Clearinghouse performs real-time, atomic ledger splits across the hierarchy (Parish 90%, Diocese 7%, Patriarchate 3%), supporting TELEBIRR, CBE BIRR, Stripe, and manual cash gateway entries.
